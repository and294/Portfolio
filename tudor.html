Tudor Black-Bay 58
page redesign

The idea was to redesign the product page for the Tudor Black-Bay 58 in order to first talk about the history of the creation of the model that led to the heavy inspirations and the name of this watch line.

This would allow watch enthusiasts to read and see pictures of the vintage models as well as educate newcomers on why these watches bear that vintage esthetic.

The main part displays every watches in the order of which they came out with the pricing and the different straps and bracelet options.

Finally, since it is not necessarily what may be of interest for the casual watch buyer, the last part is a description of the movement as well as one of the bracelets and straps.
